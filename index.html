What social media app will eventually take over?

    Research #1 Instagram vs. Snapchat. Snapchat and Instagram are two different media platforms with the same purpose. On Snapchat you take and send photos to friends and you can add text/color to them. Instagram is more like Facebook but only images and videos are posted and displayed.

    Research #2 Instagram vs. Twitter. Social media engagement for Instagram basically begins and ends in the app. But, with Twitter you can drive engagement outside of the social network byt getting people to Tweet links to content peoples websistes and other platforms.

    Research #3 Twitter vs. Snapchat. While Snapchat recently surpassed Twitter in daily active users, the two networks offer very different services to users. Snapchat has content that users are interested in right now but Twitter has more revenue models.
